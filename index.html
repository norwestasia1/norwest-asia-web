Who We Are

Norwegian Heritage. Asian Reach. Engineering Discipline.

Norwest Asia is the Asian operating arm of Norwest Marine Service AS, a Norwegian marine and industrial engineering company established in Avaldsnes, Western Norway. Through our affiliation with the Norway-based head office, we serve as the official QuantiParts distributor for the Asian region — part of a global network specializing in genuine and OEM-equivalent spare parts for legacy and medium-speed diesel engines.

Our parent company holds a Delphi/Experian credit score of 897/1000 and is ISPS certified by the Norwegian Coastal Administration (Kystverket) for port facility security compliance — credentials that reflect operational discipline and financial reliability.

What We Do

Five Core Capabilities

We do not hold a static product catalogue — the range is too vast. Instead, we maintain a comprehensive brand and platform reference, and we identify the exact part you need from your engine data, OEM number, or dimensional specification.

01

Genuine & OEM-Equivalent Spare Parts Supply

We source and deliver critical spare parts for medium-speed and heavy-duty engines across marine, power generation, mining, construction, and industrial sectors worldwide.

  • Cylinder liners, piston crowns, piston rings, connecting rods
  • Main bearings, conrod bearings, thrust bearings, camshaft bearings
  • Fuel injection pumps, injectors, nozzles, high-pressure pipes
  • Cylinder heads, valve seats, valve guides, valve springs, rotators
  • Gasket sets (top/bottom), O-rings, lip seals, manifold gaskets
  • Lube oil pumps, fuel transfer pumps, cooling water pumps, bilge pumps
  • Complete overhaul kits (partial or full)
  • Filtration systems — lube oil, fuel, air intake, hydraulic
  • Separator components (Westfalia, Alfa Laval)
  • Turbocharger cartridges, compressor wheels, nozzle rings
  • Ignition coils, spark plugs, pre-chamber components (gas engines)
  • Hydraulic pumps, motors, cylinders, seals, and control valves
02

Technical Cross-Referencing & Parts Identification

Engine serial number worn? Nameplate missing? Part number obsolete? Our engineering team identifies the correct replacement from multiple data sources.

  • Engine nameplate data and serial numbers
  • Photographs, dimensional measurements, and sample analysis
  • OEM technical drawings and microfiche archives
  • Reverse engineering for obsolete components
  • Cross-reference to modern equivalents
03

On-Site Technical Support & Engineering Deployment

Our engineering teams are globally deployable at short notice for troubleshooting, commissioning, overhaul supervision, and training.

  • On-site troubleshooting and fault diagnosis
  • Commissioning of new engines and auxiliary systems

  • Condition assessment and maintenance planning
  • Technical advisory for fleet-wide parts consolidation

Obsolete, Hard-to-Source & Legacy Part Solutions
